Initiatives

Team Hustlers

Team 1 + 3

We are a collaborated team with teams 1 & 3! What we do is support physical wellness to our audience whether it is motivating people to become physically fit or giving workouts online for people to follow! We want people to be able to feel comfortable and healthy with their own body because taking care of yourself is really important! This is beneficial to our audience because we give motivation so that they stay productive and use their time better. The goal is to make everyone feel physically healthy and overall stay more productive with their life. Some potential upcoming events to watch out for from our team could be a guest speaker who then explains why it’s important to stay physically fit or maybe a live workout where everyone can workout together!

Connect with CREATE

Team 2 + 4

Connect with Create aims to promote social wellness through meaningful connections in our community. With social media platforms such as Discord and Instagram, individuals can branch out into discovering new hobbies, building new friendships, and joining a welcoming environment with people similar to you! Social Wellness is something that we find very important in today's world amid current circumstances revolving around the pandemic. With social wellness, it is important to ensure that individuals have supportive connections that are significant through these tough times. Our first event will be a Valentine’s Day Movie Night on Saturday, Feb 12th and it will be hosted on our discord server! It provides a chance for our community that we have built, to come together and bond over a past time that everyone enjoys! After that, we hope to plan more events that will promote the overall well-being of social wellness!

Studio CREATE

Team 5 + 7

Studio Create aka Teams 5 and 7 have joined together to engage our community through creative wellness! We plan to have monthly prompts on our instagram @5tudio_crea7e to give students an outlet to display any form of art! They can submit paintings, photography pieces, dances, cooking, and more! In the spring, our team hopes to host a cultural event where students can come together and learn more about the different types of artwork, food, and history in each culture!! Stay tuned… it’s going to be exciting :)!!!

Team BEATE

Team 6

Hello, we're team 6: also known as BEATE! BEATE stands for Better Environmental Actions Towards Everyone, which is what we are all about. Team 6 strives to give students ways to understand, improve and create better ways to help contribute to a better Earth, environment and community. We aim for education and awareness in minimizing ones carbon footprint - especially towards teens and peers our age. We are constantly striving for practical sustainability, and looking for ways on how to have a better impact on the environment. Through posts on social media, we will provide information to others with easily digestible information. Some future events to look forward to will include online conferences and social media events! The online conference will focus on ways to lessen your environmental footprint in a less disruptive way, along with much else. Please stay tuned on as we will be posting more details on our Instagram (@teambeate).

LEAFE

Team 8

LEAFE (Leading Environmental Actions For Everyone), is a student-led non-profit organization that focuses on influencing positive environmental action and sparking initiative amongst their community. Join LEAFE on their journey by listening to their podcast where they share their genuine conversations and lighthearted thoughts about having an environmentally friendly lifestyle. They cover a variety of different subjects, with each topic relating to how communities are able to make an impact on the world, no matter how significant. If podcasts are not your thing, then tune in to their Youtube channel (LEAFE), where they invite you to laugh and smile through their entertaining content. All the while, check out their Instagram @leafe.create for simple posts and bi-weekly reminders to be more conscious of the environment. Watch out for a possible garbage pick-up event later in the year, where individuals can earn prizes and experience the feeling of joy by helping the community take one step closer to having a cleaner future! While LEAFE makes an impact, they hope to promote others to do the same :)
